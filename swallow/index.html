swallow offers fully capable 48% design, on an ergonomic ortholinear layout.

3 layers: switch plate, PCB and bottom plate. Equipped with Kailh hotswap sockets , swallow is ready for working on the go.

swallow is available in two colors choices: Black, and White. Current target price is under $60 for a full DIY kit. Shipping is available worldwide with tracking in most countries

swallow is in late beta testing phase, with fully working prototypes produced and teseted. Next plans are: IC for feature requests/acrylic case
